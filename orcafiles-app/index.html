❓ Help & How-To

📁 How do I add files?
Click + Add Files in the top bar. Your browser will ask you to select a folder — OrcaFiles will scan it and show all your files. Nothing is uploaded anywhere; everything stays on your computer.
🔒 How do I encrypt a file?
Right-click any file and choose Encrypt File, or set your Default Protection Level to "Encrypted" in Settings. OrcaFiles uses AES-256-GCM encryption — your key never leaves your device.
🤖 How do I use AI tagging?
Right-click a file and choose AI Tag, or click the 🤖 button in the file details panel. AI tagging uses Lightchain AIVM and requires a Home or Pro plan (or you have 5 free uses to start).
💬 How do I use Ask Keiko (AI chat)?
Click the Ask AI button in the bottom right corner. You can ask things like "find all my PDFs", "which files haven't been backed up?", or "summarize this document." Requires a Home or Pro plan.
⭐ What's the difference between plans?
Free — 5 AI uses, all basic features. Home ($1.99/mo) — unlimited AI, all personal features. Pro ($4.99/mo) — everything in Home plus Watch Folders and Training Module. Payment is in LCAI via Lightchain — no credit cards needed.
👁️ What are Watch Folders? (Pro)
Watch Folders automatically monitor a folder on your computer and tag or encrypt new files as they arrive. Available on the Pro plan.
💾 How does backup work?
Go to Backup & Restore in the sidebar and click Back Up Now. Your browser will ask you to pick a destination folder — choose your internal drive, an external hard drive, or a thumb drive. OrcaFiles copies all your files there along with a catalog file. You can then drag that folder into Google Drive, Dropbox, or any cloud service yourself. No accounts or sign-ins needed inside OrcaFiles.
🔑 How do I connect my wallet?
Click Connect Wallet in the top bar. Make sure you have Trust Wallet installed and are on the Lightchain network (chain ID 9200). Your wallet is used to pay for your subscription in LCAI.
